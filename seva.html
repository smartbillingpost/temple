Seva Descriptions

Mahanyasapurvaka Ekadasa Rudrabhisekam

Powerful abhishekam with Vedic chanting of Rudram for spiritual purification and blessings.

Chandi Yagam & Chandiparayanam

Performed to invoke Divine Mother Durga for protection and prosperity.

Rudra Homan & Rudra Yagam

Sacred fire ritual dedicated to Lord Shiva for removing obstacles.

Rahu Kethu Pooja

Special pooja performed on Sundays to reduce planetary doshas.

Kumkuma Pooja

Devotional offering to Goddess Annapurna seeking family prosperity.

Swamy Vari Kalyanam

Divine wedding ceremony of Lord Shiva and Goddess Annapurna.

Bala Pooja

Prayer offered for children's well-being and good health.

Suhashini Pooja

Respectful worship of married women symbolizing divine feminine power.

Vahana Pooja

Blessings for new vehicles for safety and prosperity.

Shanthi Pooja

Performed to remove negative energies and bring peace.

Namakaranam (Barasala)

Traditional naming ceremony performed for newborn babies.

Other Poojas

Devotees can request customized poojas based on spiritual needs.
